# SPOTLIGHT

**Discover future cultural icons before everyone else.**

SPOTLIGHT is a creator intelligence and discovery platform designed to identify emerging artists, creators, athletes, streamers, and cultural trends before they reach mainstream adoption.

Inspired by the best elements of Spotify discovery, Bloomberg intelligence, and modern consumer product design, SPOTLIGHT helps users uncover rising talent through momentum signals, community activity, cultural relevance, and real-world creator data.

---

## Vision

The internet has millions of creators competing for attention.

SPOTLIGHT exists to answer a simple question:

**Who's next?**

Our goal is to build the platform where fans, creators, brands, and industry insiders discover future icons before the rest of the world.

## Product Direction

SPOTLIGHT is currently focused on creator discovery, momentum tracking, and cultural intelligence.

The platform is intentionally designed around discovering emerging creators rather than financial speculation.

The long-term vision includes deeper creator ecosystem tooling, creator analytics, discovery infrastructure, and community-driven cultural intelligence.

---

## Why I Built This

As both a product builder and technology enthusiast, I was fascinated by the idea that the next global creator often exists long before mainstream recognition.

SPOTLIGHT explores how data, community insight, storytelling, and product design can help surface emerging talent earlier and create a better discovery experience for fans and creators alike.
